Let's Cook Delicious Sparghetti In Minutes!

Welcome to my recipe page.

Here you will learn everything you need to know about how to cook delicious spaghetti.

Our Recipe Information

  1. Preparation Time: 10-15 minutes.
  2. Cooking Time: 8-15 minutes.
  3. Serving: 4-6 people (depending on serving size).
  4. Difficulty: Beginner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti.
  • Salt, for the boiling water.
  • Tomato sauce.
  • Pepper.
  • Maggie.
  • Seaseonings.

Instructions

  1. Boil water: Fill a large pot with salted water (about 4-6 quarts of water with 1-2 tablespoons of Salt).
  2. Cook spaghetti: Add the spaghetti to the boiling water. Cook according to the package instructions (usually about 8-10 minutes).
  3. Check doneness: Taste the spaghetti to ensure it's cooked to ur liking. It should have a bit of a bite.
  4. Drain: Reserve about 1 cup pasta water and then drain the spaghetti in a sieve.
  5. Maggie: Add 2-3 cubes of maggie.
  6. pepper: Add 1-2 tablespoons of pepper.
  7. Seaseonings:Add Your favorite Seaseoning mixed with tomatos cook for 4-5 minutes.
  8. Add tomato sauce: Return the spaghetti to the pot or a large serving bowl. Add your tomato sauce and toss to combine. If the sauce is too thick, add some reserved pasta water.
  9. serve: Serve hot, topped with chicken lap and other desired toppings.

Tips:

  • Use a large pot to prevent the spaghetti from becoming tangled.
  • Salt the water generously to flavor the pasta.
  • Reserve some pasta water to adjust the sauce consistency.
  • Don't overcook the spaghetti.
